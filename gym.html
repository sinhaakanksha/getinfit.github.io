Simple Exercises For Beginners:

☛ SPOT JOGGING

The best way to burn calorise is jogging. But if you are too busy to go out you can go for spot jogging. Ajogging of 10 min burns upto 50 calories. So start with 8-10 min of jogging.


☛ SKIPPING

Skipping needs no introduction.Its more of fun than exercise. Skipping burns upto 10 calories per minute. So start with 8-10 min of skipping daily.


☛ SQUARTS

Squats are a great way to lose inches from thighs, but it is also great to lose overall body weight. It increases the heartbeat and gives a feel of the cardiovascular exercise. Stand with your feet shoulders width apart and squat in a sitting position. Your thighs should be parallel to the floor and back straight. If you find this workout difficult, use a chair for support. 100 squats help you burn close to 108 calories



☛ CRUNCHES

Abdominal fat is one problem that everybody wants to get rid of. The best and easiest way to do this is by doing crunches. Lay flat on your back with your knees bent. Place your hands under your hand and try to pull yourself up towards your knees. 3 sets of 25 repetitions on alternate days can do wonders to your body. 6 minutes of crunches in a day can burn up to 60 calories. The best part is that you can do it while watching television too!


☛ SIDE-LEG RISE

If you have been a lazy person all your life, then those love handles are your nightmare. Bid adieu to them by doing this very simple exercise. Just lay on either side and lift your leg as high as you can. This will help you to get rid of that stubborn fat on your sides and give you a much leaner look. You can do it easily while standing as well. 300 raises can burn up to 40 calories.


☛ PLANK

The plank exercise builds tremendous bouts of strength and is achievable from the comfort of your own home. The plank will help you build strength and with that, some serious body confidence. And we know confidence is attractive.Plank can burn upto 3-4 calories per minute. Start with 1-2 min of plank ang gradually increase the time for plank.


☛ STREACHING

Stretching not only helps you to feel relaxed, it is actually helpful in losing weight. It improves flexibility, increases blood circulation, relieves muscle tension, which contributes in fetching better results from the routine you follow. You can do it anywhere. 3 minutes of stretching after your workout are enough for visible results. Stretching for 10 minutes every day can help you get rid of almost 50 calories.
